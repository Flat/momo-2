New Music System

The music system has been remade! Now, Momo won't always be sitting in your music channel. Fear not! She will join when you queue up a song with $music. This update also brings support for Youtube playlists and Soundcloud, so go crazy with that!
  • $music supports Youtube playlists links and Soundcloud
  • $music volume (0-100) sets the volume for your music. Needs the kick permission
  • As a sidenote, the new system allows for a lot more modularity with music sources. Basically, makes it easier for me to work with it

Pokemon command

Pokemon command! Search up any pokemon from gen 1-6 (including alternate forms, i.e. meloetta-aria) and display some useful stats
  • $pokemon blaziken searches for Blaziken...

Tutorial command

A new command joins the fray! $howto is a new way to learn how to use certain bot features. It's useful, especially for new servers

Configuration command

v1.1 (December 31, 2016): New updates! Click each heading to get more information

Reddit Integration

Reddit feeds: Get new posts from your designated subreddits delivered straight to your channel. With options to show or hide images (nsfw or all), self-posts previews, this integration will surely upgrade your server to the next level.
Note: Posts with the word "spoiler" in the title will never show images!
Note 2: Posts have a 30 second delay to allow users to mark their posts as NSFW or for moderators to clean up spam
  • $reddit subreddit registers a feed to your current channel. You are prompted to answer a couple questions for configuration
  • $listreddit tells you all the subreddit feeds you have and what channel they are in
  • $unreddit subreddit removes the feed from your server
  • Use cases
    • Case 1: Get fanart delivered straight to your door (/r/awwnime)
    • Case 2: Get updates for your favorite TV shows (/r/archerfx)
    • Case 3: Get nice images to brighten your day (/r/WholesomeMemes)

Twitter Integration

Twitter feeds: Get instantaneous updates from Twitter, straight to whatever channel you want. With an option to show or hide images, this slick integration will keep you updated
  • $twitter twitter-handle registers a feed to your current channel. You are prompted to answer an answer for configuration
  • $listtwitter tells you all the twitter feeds you have and what channel they are in
  • $untwitter twitter-handle removes the feed from your server
  • Use cases
    • Case 1: Get news on global events (@BBCNews)
    • Case 2: Get updates from your favorite streamer (@TrumpSC)
    • Case 3: ... I'm not this creative

Youtube Command

Directly search Youtube! Sounds simple... but! You can also use the $music command to directly play these results! Cool!
  • $youtube search query searches Youtube for... your search query! Returns a numbered list which you can then...
  • $music # plays that Youtube video in the music channel. Double cool!

Prune Command

Spam? Bad images? Unsightly arguments? Look no further than the prune command
  • $prune # prunes the past # number of messages. Limit of 75 max. If you don't specify a #, default to 40
  • $prune @target prunes messages sent by target user. This searches back 40 total messages and deletes their messages. Not just the target's!
  • $prune # @target prunes messages sent by target user. This searches back # total messages - not just the target's!
